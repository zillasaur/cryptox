About Us


We start off with a new world.

In this world, CryptoX users define what makes up the economy. The first decision, made by the founder of the world, is to decide what the world's base currency will be. Wolds can have any crypto currency as their foundation, and 30% of it's economy is allocated to the base currency.


A diversified economy is created.

The founder then allocates the other 70% of the world's economy into other investment vehicles including stocks, bonds, currencies, and other crypto currencies. The diversity of the world's economy is shown through the establishments on the world.






All worlds are open ended.

As other users join the world they take on ownership of the world's economy for the amount they decided to invest. Changes in the allocation of resources can be suggested by any world member, and changes are made when the community comes to concences.




Change in ownership.

When a user decides to leave, they are paid out for their ownership. Users can join and sell ownership at any time.
